Recon (Information Gathering)

Recon means collecting information before testing a system.

Hackers don’t attack blindly. They first study the target.

They look for: - website structure - public data - possible weak points

Real-life 🌍: Before hacking a website, a hacker learns how it works.

This step is important for both attackers and defenders.
