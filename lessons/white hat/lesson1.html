What is White Hat?

White hat hackers are ethical hackers who help protect systems instead of breaking them.

They have the same skills as hackers, but they use them for good.

Think of them as: - Digital police 👮 - Security testers 🛡️

Real-life 🌍: Companies hire white hats to find bugs before criminals do.

They don’t steal data. They report problems and help fix them.
