What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity means protecting computers, phones, and websites from hackers.

Think of your phone like a locker 🔐. Inside it you store photos, chats, passwords, and personal data.

If someone breaks into it, they can: - steal your data - access your social accounts - even take money from your bank apps

Cybersecurity works like: - 🔒 locks (passwords) - 🛡️ guards (security systems) - 🚨 alarms (alerts when something is wrong)

Real-life example 🌍: Using "123456" as password is like leaving your door open.

Big companies spend millions to protect their systems.
