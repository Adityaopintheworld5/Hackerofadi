HTML: Headings & Paragraphs

Think of headings as titles in a book and paragraphs as the text under each title. Headings h1-h6 show importance; paragraphs p show details.

Example:

<h1>My Favorite Food</h1>
<p>Pizza is my favorite because it is cheesy and tasty.</p>
